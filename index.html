Education

  • I graduated from Temasek Polytechnic with a Food Science and Nutrition Diploma in 2021, and had made the decision to take up Culinary Arts at the Culinary Institute of America (CIA)/Singapore Institute of Technology (SIT). However, I withdrew from this course after my first year due to a clash of values and I have decided to pursue my passion - fashion. I was taught how to sew and operate a sewing machine by my mother during the Circuit Breaker in June 2020. Subsequently, I searched up and followed sewing tutorials for clothes on Youtube and started on upcycling projects. The following year (2021) while I was waiting for my first semester at SIT to start, I tried my hand at crocheting and have been involved in my own projects until now. After I stopped school, I started working full time at a thrift store called "Thrift Label" to earn some income and picked up sewing again to build on my portfolio. Seeing that web design was a relevant skill for this course, I taught myself how to code and design my own websites with softwares like Brackets and Visual Studio Code. This website was coded using Brackets. I am currently learning how to use softwares like Photoshop and am actively using canva, Picsarts and Meitu for my posters and editorials that were part of my portfolio. I enjoy visiting museums and window-shopping in my free time!
